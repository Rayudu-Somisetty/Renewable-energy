What is Solar Energy?

How it works: Sunlight strikes photovoltaic cells. Electrons get energized and create direct current, which an inverter converts to usable alternating current.

Advantages:

  • Renewable and infinite source
  • Reduces carbon emissions
  • Low operating costs
  • Eco-friendly

Limitations:

  • Weather dependent (clouds/rain)
  • High initial installation cost
  • Requires space
